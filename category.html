Category of medicine

Pharmacy Medicine Categories

Pain Killer (Analgesics)

Use: Dard, bukhar, headache, body pain
Example Medicines: Panadol, Brufen, Disprin
How it works: Pain signals ko kam karta hai
Common Forms: Tablet, syrup
Notes: Zyada use se stomach issue ho sakta hai

Antibiotic

Use: Bacterial infection ka ilaj
Example Medicines: Augmentin
How it works: Bacteria ko khatam karta hai
Common Forms: Tablet, capsule, injection
Notes: Doctor ki advice zaroori hoti hai

Syrup

Use: Cough, flu, throat infection
Example Medicines: Cough Syrup
How it works: Throat ko soothe karta hai
Common Forms: Liquid
Notes: Children ke liye easy hota hai

Injection

Use: Serious illness, diabetes
Example Medicines: Insulin
How it works: Direct blood mein effect karta hai
Common Forms: Injectable
Notes: Trained person se lagwana chahiye

Antacid

Use: Acidity, gas, heartburn
Example Medicines: Rennie, Gaviscon
How it works: Acid ko neutral karta hai
Common Forms: Tablet, liquid
Notes: Overuse avoid karein
